ReAct Reasoning Template
=========================

Use this format for each step of the reasoning process:

---
Thought: {Describe your reasoning. What do you know so far? What do you need to find out next?}
Action: {The tool name to call — must be one of: get_course_info, get_prerequisite, calculate_learning_time, generate_learning_plan}
Action Input: {JSON object with tool parameters}
---

After receiving the tool result:

---
Observation: {The tool's output}
---

Then repeat with another Thought → Action → Observation cycle.

When you have all necessary information for a complete answer:

---
Thought: I now have all the information needed to create the learning plan.
Final Answer: {Structured output following the format specified in the system prompt}
---

## Example Trace

User Input: course="Python", daily_hours=3, duration_days=12

---
Thought: 首先需要查询 Python 的课程信息，确认课程存在并获取学时、模块和先修要求。
Action: get_course_info
Action Input: {"course_name": "Python"}
---

---
Observation: {"course_name": "Python", "estimated_total_hours": 24, "difficulty": "beginner", "prerequisites": [], "topics": [...]}
---

---
Thought: Python 无先修要求。24h总学时。接下来检查先修条件。
Action: get_prerequisite
Action Input: {"course_name": "Python", "user_knowledge": []}
---

---
Observation: {"satisfied": true, "missing": [], "total_prerequisite_hours": 0}
---

---
Thought: 先修满足。现在评估时间可行性: 3h/天 × 12天 = 36h可用 vs 24h需要。
Action: calculate_learning_time
Action Input: {"course_name": "Python", "daily_hours": 3, "duration_days": 12}
---

---
Observation: {"feasible": true, "available_hours": 36, "course_total_hours": 24, "buffer_hours": 12}
---

---
Thought: 可行，12h缓冲。现在生成完整学习计划。
Action: generate_learning_plan
Action Input: {"course_name": "Python", "daily_hours": 3, "duration_days": 12, "skip_optional": false, "start_date": null}
---

... (continue until complete)
